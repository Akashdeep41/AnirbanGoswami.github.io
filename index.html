ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Anirban Goswami

Bankura

I am a dedicated social worker and political activist. Right now, I am the District President of SFI and a member of the CPI(M) Bankura District Committee. The soil of Bankura is where my journey began. I work hard to strengthen the link between grassroots struggles and progressive social change. I work hard to give students, workers, and marginalized groups a voice because I believe in equality and justice. I think that real social change needs more than just strong organization and working together; it also needs creative communication that inspires people and gets society moving. In addition to my political and social work, I also work as a creator and designer. I use visual storytelling and powerful campaigns to raise awareness, fight injustice, and encourage people to stand together. My work shows a constant effort to combine ideology, activism and creativity. For example, I lead movements on the streets, organize community relief efforts, and come up with meaningful public campaigns.
